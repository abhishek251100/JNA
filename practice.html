Real Estate Practice

The Real Estate practice of the Firm includes Title verification of properties and Conveyancing of Land and Apartments and ancillary matters and registration of documents. The Firm also advises on Leasing and Licensing of properties and preparation of documentation for the same.

INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

Expertise in trademarks, patents, design, and copyright registration. Handles litigation, prosecution, and business transactions like licensing, franchising, and joint ventures. Clients include entertainment, IT companies, and digital content providers.

GENERAL CORPORATE COMMERCIAL

Advises on investments, alliances, and compliance. Services include structuring investment vehicles, joint ventures, technology transfer, documentation, and corporate legal affairs like contracts, employee stock options, benefits, and employment matters, including termination and disciplinary actions.

PRIVATE EQUITY

Offers advice on structuring, due diligence, approvals, negotiation, and documentation. Supports investee companies in diverse sectors. Expertise includes exit strategy planning and transactional assistance from term sheet stage to closure.

TAXATION

Advises on excise, customs, service tax, VAT, and GST compliance. Represents clients before authorities including CESTAT, VAT Tribunals, High Courts, and the Supreme Court, ensuring effective navigation of regulatory challenges.

MERGERS & ACQUISITIONS

Specializes in mergers, acquisitions, divestments, and restructuring for listed and unlisted entities. Clients include large industrial houses and multinationals. Collaborates with in-house counsels, investment banks, and accounting firms for seamless transaction execution.

JHANGIANI NARULA & ASSOCIATES

Established in 1999, Jhangiani, Narula & Associates is a renowned legal firm listed with the Bombay High Court. With offices in Delhi and Mumbai, the firm specializes in intellectual property, litigation, real estate, and conflict resolution. Its extensive range of legal services includes conveyance, real estate transactions, financial consulting, mergers, and cross-border legal matters. Through its network of partners, the firm provides clients across India with expert legal counsel.
