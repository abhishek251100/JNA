About Us

Established in 1999, Jhangiani, Narula & Associates is a renowned legal practice that is listed with the Bombay High Court. The business, which has offices in Delhi and Mumbai, focuses on intellectual property, litigation, real estate, and conflict resolution. Conveyance, real estate transactions, financial consulting, mergers, and cross-border legal issues are among its extensive range of legal services. The business provides customers all throughout India with knowledgeable legal advice thanks to its network of partners.
