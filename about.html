About Us

Jhangiani, Narula & Associates, founded by Mr. Dilip Jhangiani and Mr. Rajiv Narula in the year 1999 and has been registered as a Law Firm with the Bombay High Court on the Original Side since that year. The Firm has, thereafter, seen the inclusion of Mr. Anuuj N Narula and Mr.Aurup Dasgupta as Partners.

The Firm has been actively practicing in litigation undertaken in the Bombay High Court and Bombay City Civil Court at Mumbai and Goregaon and any other ancillary Courts and Tribunals in Mumbai. The Firm has offices in downtown Mumbai at Churchgate and Colaba as well as in Goregaon in the suburbs.

The Firm commenced legal practice in Delhi in the year 2007 under the name J. N. A. Law with Delhi-based Partners.

The Delhi office has been active in litigation practice in the Supreme Court, Delhi High Court and other ancillary Courts and Tribunals in Delhi and has its office located in Central Delhi in proximity to the Delhi High Court and Supreme Court.

The Firm works with Associates in other major Cities in India on litigation matters. Although real estate, litigation and dispute resolution are the Firm’s core practice areas, the Firm has a substantial practice and experience in Conveyancing and Property transactions, as well as advising clients on investment transactions, both domestic and cross-border and on amalgamation mergers and restructuring of Companies.

The Firm also undertakes work in the Intellectual Property Rights area and advises clients on matters relating to Copyrights, Trademarks and Patents.

JHANGIANI NARULA & ASSOCIATES

Established in 1999, Jhangiani, Narula & Associates is a renowned legal firm listed with the Bombay High Court. With offices in Delhi and Mumbai, the firm specializes in intellectual property, litigation, real estate, and conflict resolution. Its extensive range of legal services includes conveyance, real estate transactions, financial consulting, mergers, and cross-border legal matters. Through its network of partners, the firm provides clients across India with expert legal counsel.
